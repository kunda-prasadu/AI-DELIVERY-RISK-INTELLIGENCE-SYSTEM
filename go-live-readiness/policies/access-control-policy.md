# Access Control Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to all services in the AI Delivery Risk Intelligence platform.

## Controls

- Role-based access control is enforced through service-level authorization checks.
- MFA requirement applies to administrative access in production environments.
- Quarterly access review is required for privileged roles and service accounts.

## Enforcement

- Unauthorized requests are denied and logged in audit telemetry.
- Access exceptions require approval from Security and Platform leads.
