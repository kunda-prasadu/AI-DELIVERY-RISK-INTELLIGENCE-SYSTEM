# Data Retention Policy

## Principles

- Data minimization is required for all collected user and operational fields.
- Only required operational and compliance data may be retained.

## Retention windows

- Security and audit data: retained according to release and compliance obligations.
- Operational metrics: retained based on observability and incident-analysis needs.

## Deletion workflow

- Expired records are removed through bounded retention routines.
- Deletion operations must be auditable and included in release evidence.
